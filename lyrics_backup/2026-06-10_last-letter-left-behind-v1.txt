[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I left the ink upon the page for you to find
A midnight whisper in the fading light
I leave the shadows and the world behind
To hold you closer in the warm and quiet night

[Verse 1]
The coffee cools upon the wooden frame
The embers glow and catch a steady fire
I hear the wind begin to call your name
And fill the quiet with a soft desire

[Pre-Chorus]
Step across the room and take my hand
Let the hours melt into the floor
This is the moment that we understand
There is a feeling we can’t ignore

[Chorus]
Oh, this is the last letter left behind
Written in the way I look at you
A slow confession running through my mind
And every promise that I hold on to

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, a slow confession pulling through
Just pulling closer into you

[Verse 2]
The midnight settles on the open road
But we are sheltered in a tender space
I drop the weight of every heavy load
Just to get lost inside your warm embrace

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
The words are written on the window pane
I feel the rhythm like a quiet rain
A gentle pull that we no longer hide
I give you everything I keep inside
I drop the walls that keep us so apart
I am Saeed, and you have all my heart

[Spoken]
